POI Research Pathway

Decision framework for selecting the right research design based on existing evidence for a Practice of Interest (POI)

* In some cases it may be appropriate to proceed with a Hybrid Type I trial in the absence of effectiveness evidence — e.g., very strong efficacy, indirect evidence supportive of potential effectiveness in context of interest, and/or strong momentum supporting implementation in a health care context.
